# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be respectful. Assume good faith. Keep discussion focused on the work.

## Expected behavior

- Treat everyone with respect, regardless of background or experience level.
- Give and accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users.
- Respect differing viewpoints; disagree on substance, not on people.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind.
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior.
- Publishing anyone's private information without their explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior by opening an issue or contacting a maintainer privately.
Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled fairly. Maintainers may remove content,
comments, or contributors that violate this code.
